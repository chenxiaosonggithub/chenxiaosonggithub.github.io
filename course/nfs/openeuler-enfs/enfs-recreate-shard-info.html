1 需求描述

执行以下操作后重新生成shard信息:

mount -t nfs -o vers=3,localaddrs=192.168.53.209~192.168.53.52,remoteaddrs=10.42.43.92~10.42.43.93 10.42.43.92:/enfs-test /mnt/
modprobe -r enfs
modprobe enfs
