Recording 1a 
Speaker A: On Mondays at school a group of us always talk about whatever movies we saw at the weekend. On Saturdays I often get together with my classmates and we see all the latest releases together.Ican't remember the last time I saw a film with my parents - we just don't have much in common any more.
Speaker B: My parents are both teachers so you'd imagine I'd have no trouble at all academically. When I was little it was great because we had a really great relationship. But nowadays all we seem to do is argue and that causes a lot of conflict between us, so I don't really feel I can go to them for help. My friends aren't much help either as they've all got the same problem. Thank goodness I get on really well with my tutor at university. She's very approachable and, if I'm struggling with an assignment, I find her advice really helps me.
Speaker C: My parents are quite old so I feel as though they're out of
touch with the modern world. They don't seem to have any idea of what things cost. I'm hoping to get a car in the next few months but l'll be taking my older brother along to help. We used to fight a lot when we were growing up but there's a really close bond between us now.He's already had a few cars so I'm sure he'll be a great help.
Speaker D: I play the violin and the piano and my grandad is a great cello
player. A lot of my friends at school listen to all the popular bands and singers, but my tastes are totally different. I prefer classical music and they just don't understand it at all. Luckily Grandad shares my taste, so we often buy CDs and talk about them together.

说话人 A：
在学校，每周一我们一群人总是会聊周末看过的电影。周六我经常和同学们聚在一起，一起去看最新上映的电影。我都记不起上一次和父母一起看电影是什么时候了——我们现在几乎没有什么共同点了。
说话人 B：
我的父母都是老师，所以你可能会以为我在学习上完全没有问题。我小时候情况很好，因为我们关系非常亲密。但现在我们似乎总是在争吵，这在我们之间造成了很多冲突，所以我不太觉得可以向他们寻求帮助。我的朋友们也帮不上什么忙，因为他们都有同样的问题。幸好我和大学导师关系很好。她很平易近人，如果我在作业上遇到困难，她的建议对我帮助很大。
说话人 C：
我的父母年纪比较大，所以我觉得他们跟不上现代社会。他们似乎对物价一无所知。我希望在接下来的几个月里买一辆车，但我会带上我哥哥一起去帮忙。我们小时候经常打架，但现在彼此之间有很深的感情。他已经拥有过几辆车了，所以我相信他会帮上大忙。
说话人 D：
我会拉小提琴和弹钢琴，我的爷爷是个很棒的大提琴演奏者。学校里的很多朋友都听流行乐队和歌手的音乐，但我的品味完全不同。我更喜欢古典音乐，而他们根本无法理解。幸运的是，爷爷和我有相同的爱好，所以我们经常一起买CD并讨论音乐。
